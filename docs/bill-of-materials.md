# Bill of materials

Core parts:

- Arduino Nano 5 V / ATmega328P;
- 0-12 bar pressure sensor, 0.5-4.5 V output, 5 V supply;
- JST XH 2.54 mm 10-pin male/female connectors for a reversible harness;
- compatible hydraulic fittings for the added pressure sensor branch;
- short PTFE tubes or suitable high-pressure coffee-machine tubing;
- O-rings and seals suitable for hot water / coffee-machine use;
- heat shrink, insulation, cable ties, and mounting hardware.

Optional / useful:

- spare connectors for testing harnesses;
- multimeter;
- oscilloscope or logic analyzer, used only with proper isolation and safe measurement practices;
- blind basket for pressure testing;
- notebook for recording pressure behavior and firmware settings.

## Connector reminder

Use JST XH 2.54 mm connectors. Do not label them as 2.5 mm in the documentation or parts list.
