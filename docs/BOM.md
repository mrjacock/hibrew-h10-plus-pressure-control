## Recommended tools

- multimeter;
- battery-powered oscilloscope or isolated measurement setup;
- soldering iron;
- wire stripper;
- heat gun;
- small wrenches;
- food-safe thread sealing solution only if appropriate for the fitting type.

## Fitting warning

Do not assume that all `G1/8` parts are mechanically interchangeable in a compact plastic fitting. Thread length, shoulder geometry, O-ring seat, and sealing method matter.

The best solution is to use mechanically compatible fittings and seals. Do not rely on excessive thread tape to compensate for incorrect parts.
